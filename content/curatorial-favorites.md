# brain

### well designed tools for organizing your thoughts and navigating the internet

- [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/) to create an interconnected index of thoughts and ideas
- [are.na](https://www.are.na/) (esp. with the browser extension) for a low friction way to collect and organize links, articles, ideas, images, etc
- the [Brave](https://brave.com/) browser - it has been years since I’ve seen a YouTube ad

### writers

# related to my work in climate and energy research

## podcasts

#### climate & energy

- [Catalyst](https://open.spotify.com/show/4C5Qx3wJn0GeTnDxIVYcAR?si=fbe3942364b64d54) - interesting conversations on energy tech & systems from a VC
- [Switched On](https://open.spotify.com/show/5P3uXhpfC8kSLVCRRkgGnj?si=581cdb6d8cad4982) - deep on. energy transition topics
- [Critical Capital](https://open.spotify.com/show/1SgET3FAhi7Jmk3VF0onAT?si=1064c66e5fb742db) - newer, interesting stories from energy & markets
- [Stepchange](https://open.spotify.com/show/3RHJ129caYkxh2FliqtaBP?si=3a5dca820a5b4309) - newer, like Acquired for energy systems

#### business/econ

- [Odd Lots](https://open.spotify.com/show/1te7oSFyRVekxMBJUSethH?si=9e82df2424a540c1) - wide ranging and extremely interesting stories related to the economy and financial markets
- [Eye on the Market](https://open.spotify.com/show/1nV00H9U4FlMWpBKC1proP?si=1404d5a6ac514f85) - interesting research on macro econ topics
- [Acquired](https://open.spotify.com/show/7Fj0XEuUQLUqoMZQdsLXqp?si=d4d9e4ff91a74431) - deeply researched stories how people built companies

## newsletters

- [Polycrisis](https://phenomenalworld.org/newsletter/the-polycrisis/) from Tim Sahay & Kate Mackenzie - leftist geopolitics and climate
- [Kyla’s Newsletter](https://kyla.substack.com/) by Kyla Scanlon - interesting econ analysis with generational resonance
- [Steel for Fuel](https://steelforfuel.substack.com/) by Andy Lubershane - interesting technology and economic takes from the head of research at a climate/energy VC

# physical/tactile/sensory

- having some form of movement practice that makes your body a better home for you (more comfortable, stronger, more capable, less prone to injury)
  - specifically, I have really enjoyed yoga - would recommend [Charlie Follows](https://www.youtube.com/@CharlieFollows) on Youtube and [Ritual House](https://ritualhouseseattle.com/) is my favorite in-person yoga studio in Seattle
- spas (especially those with saunas, cold plunge, pools of water)
- aesop hand balm in eleos - just smells genuinely amazing

# misc

- [ThriftBooks](https://www.thriftbooks.com/) for getting cheap used books
