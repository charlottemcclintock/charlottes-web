### blue corduroy coat for Rohit

this was my second big sewing project on my new machine after taking an incredible class on making overalls with Emelia at [Summit Selvage](https://summitselvage.com/). Rohit and I picked out this light blue corduroy fabric together and snagged some really incredible vintage mother of pearl buttons from [Seattle ReCreative](https://seattlerecreative.org/). this is the [Ilford Jacket](https://fridaypatterncompany.com/products/the-ilford-jacket-pdf-pattern?srsltid=AfmBOopw5wNHEkoq1WhwB49IyCaCENMf6iz8omWSeZwF8nfoM1nKVcSx) pattern from [Friday Pattery Company](https://fridaypatterncompany.com/). I found the [sew along tutorials on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9fy4rCk23LE) incredibly helpful as a new sewist. added lots of pockets, inside, outside, and on the back. 

**lessons learned:** this was my first time using bias tape and it took me a bit to get the hang of it. i ended up going back over spots i had missed in the first pass. 

### light canvas with denim pockets for me 

same pattern, faster the second time! I like the light colored canvas and used two types of leftover denim for the pockets for a bit of contrast. 

**lessons learned:** pre-wash your cotton fabric if you don’t want it to shrink! i was so excited to start working on this that I didn’t prewash my fabric, and it shrunk a little after washing.
